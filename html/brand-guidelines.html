The Big Idea

Introduction

If you're reading this, you care about SketchUp

We care about it too, so we sat down for a while to think about what makes SketchUp... SketchUp.

This brand standard site is where we write down (and draw) all the ideas and graphics that describe the SketchUp brand.

Our Story

SketchUp users are architects, designers, builders, makers and engineers. They are the people who shape the physical world. They are important, and they deserve great tools because great tools produce great work.

Great tools are ones you look forward to using. They do one thing (or maybe two) really, really well. They let you do what you want without having to figure out how. They help with hard or boring tasks so that you can focus on being creative, or productive, or both. And they are, in their own way, beautiful.

At SketchUp, we do our best to make great tools for drawing. For our users, drawing is thinking. They draw to explore ideas, to figure things out, to show other people what they mean. They draw because they love it, and because nothing great was ever built that didn't start with a great drawing.

Color Usage

Color will serve a unique role in establishing awareness and recognition of the SketchUp identity. The primary colors for reproduction of the identity are Pantone 1795 and Pantone 173-16. In addition, the identity works well in black and white. While the signature may be reproduced in one color, the two color version is preferred. The colors are represented by numbers from the Pantone Matching System® and the formulas for the four color printing process [cmyk] and for use on screen media [rgb] are provided below. The symbol and signature work best on white for the greatest level of contrast or on light Grey Pantone 420.

Voice and Style

SketchUp Voice

Personality is one outcome of a well executed brand. And because most of our users will never meet us, SketchUp’s personality is largely conveyed through writing. (Come to think of it, your’s is too) Consult the SketchUp Editorial and Style Guide to sharpen your writing and represent the brand with clarity, brevity, and authenticity.
